# Gamma Communications plc — Independent FP&A Analysis

**Prepared by: Zain | Completed 24 May 2026**

An independent financial planning & analysis project examining Gamma Communications plc (LSE: GAMA), a UK-based cloud communications company. Built using publicly available data from the company's Annual Reports (FY2022–FY2025).

## Business Question

Can Gamma's free cash flow support its debt obligations and dividend following the £144m Starface acquisition in 2025?

## Contents

- **Gamma_3-Statement_Model.xlsx** — Fully linked 3-statement model with 4 years of actuals and a 2-year forecast, including a Bear/Base/Bull scenario toggle
- **Gamma_Excel_Dashboard.xlsx** — Executive dashboard with KPI cards, segment analysis and margin trends
- **Gamma_CFO_Briefing.pdf** — One-page investment summary
- **Gamma_PowerBI_Dashboard.pbix** — Interactive Power BI dashboard
- **Gamma_PowerBI_Dashboard.jpg** — Dashboard screenshot

## Key Findings (Base Case)

- Revenue grew from £484.6m (2022) to £645.8m (2025), around 10% a year
- Adjusted EBITDA margin steady at ~22%, forecast to expand toward 23.6% by 2027
- Free cash flow of ~£64m comfortably covers interest and the £18.9m dividend
- Conclusion: Gamma can afford its debt and dividend under base case assumptions; the key risk is whether Germany keeps growing organically post-acquisition

## Note

Forecasts were prepared on 24 May 2026, prior to publication of Gamma's FY2026 results. This is an independent educational project and not investment advice.
